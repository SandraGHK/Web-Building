Do We All Irish?

Sometimes we don't know where our roots lie.
And as an American, does it really matter where our predecessors originated from?
Whether one has a proud link to the wonderful motherland, or just celebrate St. Patricks Day, it's important to understand the history behind the Irish, and how their colorful and weirdly dark history shapes Irish celebrations and holidays.

Why So Serious?

What is also interesting, is that originally, the Irish were spurned by America. Per the History Channel, Americans believed the refugees seeking haven in America were poor and disease-ridden. They threatened to take jobs away from Americans and strain welfare budgets. They practiced an alien religion and pledged allegiance to a foreign leader. They were bringing with them crime. They were accused of being rapists. And, worst of all, these undesirables were Irish.
